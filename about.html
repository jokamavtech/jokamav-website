Our Story

Who We Are

Jokamav Technologies Limited is a Nigerian-based security and smart systems solutions company, established on July 13, 2015, in Challenge, Ibadan, Oyo State.

Since our inception, we have remained committed to delivering reliable, innovative, and scalable technology solutions that enhance safety, access control, and operational efficiency for businesses, estates, and institutions across Nigeria.

From humble beginnings, Jokamav Technologies Limited has grown into a trusted name in the design, supply, installation, and maintenance of advanced security and automation systems. Our strength lies in combining technical expertise with a deep understanding of client needs.
